ABOUT US

YESS is a student-led entrepreneurship community dedicated to promote innovation and creative problem-solving. It aims to build a supportive ecosystem where students can explore ideas, experiment freely, and turn concepts into reality. Operating in an open, non-hierarchical environment, YESS values ideas based on merit and encourages collaboration, learning, and initiative.

Through workshops, discussions, competitions, and mentoring, students gain vital skills in leadership, communication, critical thinking, and project management. By combining expert guidance with hands-on experiences, YESS helps transform ideas into tangible outcomes be it prototypes, research projects, or social ventures. More than an organization, YESS is a community that embraces curiosity, collaboration, and resilience, empowering students to learn from challenges and grow as innovative thinkers and changemakers.
