MISSION

Develope a startup culture among students where ideas are explored, tested, and turned into real projects. Support collaboration, hands-on learning, and practical problem-solving. Provide guidance and resources to help students gain experience and confidence in launching initiatives

VISION

To shape a student driven ecosystem where bold ideas evolve into purposeful ventures, and learning translates into meaningful impact and opportunities
